Common Excersises

The following are common excersises that we encourage our clients to do as part of thier daily excersise routine

Burpee

Burpees are a great full body excersiseto increase your strength and indurance. Begin in standing position, drop into a squat and esxtend your hands forward, kick your feet back and then forward again quickly and then jump from a squatted position

Plank

Planks build your core strength.To perform a plank , get into a push up position and rest your forearm on the floor. hold your position as long as you can

Mountain Climbers

Mountain Climbers are a good cardio excersise , place your hand on the floor in a push up positon, then bring one knee to your chest and switch as quickly as you can ( as though you are climbing a mountain ).
